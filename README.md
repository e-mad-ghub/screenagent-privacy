# Screen Agent – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

## Overview
Screen Agent is an AI chat bubble app. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect
**We collect no personal data.** Specifically:
- No account registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting services

## Data Stored on Your Device
The following is stored locally on your device only:
- Your conversation history
- Your agent configurations (names, system prompts)
- Your API keys (stored in app preferences, never transmitted to us)

## Third-Party AI Services
When you send a message, it is transmitted directly from your device to the AI provider you configured (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, OpenRouter). We are not involved in this transmission. Please refer to the respective provider's privacy policy for how they handle your data.

## Permissions Used
- **Display over other apps** – required for the floating chat bubble
- **Foreground service** – required to keep the bubble active
- **Camera** – optional, used only if you choose to attach a photo
- **Read storage** – optional, used only if you choose to attach a file

## Contact
For questions, open an issue on this repository.
